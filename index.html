Scroll to Reveal

One image, pinned in place, clipping open frame by frame as the story scrolls past it. Built with GSAP ScrollTrigger — no build step, no framework, just honest HTML and CSS.

Scroll on

Frame 01

The pin

As you enter the gallery, the artwork locks to the centre of the screen. It stays put while the words on the left keep travelling — that steadiness is what makes the reveal feel deliberate.

Frame 02

The clip

Each new frame wipes in with a CSS clip-path inset, tied directly to your scroll position. Scroll down and it opens; scroll back and it seals shut. The motion is yours to steer.

Frame 03

The scrub

ScrollTrigger's scrub glues the timeline to the scrollbar, so the animation never runs ahead of you. Fast flick or slow crawl, the frames keep exact pace.

Frame 04

The palette

The whole stage tints toward each frame's colour as it arrives, so the background breathes along with the images. Four frames, one continuous, scroll-driven gradient.

That's the trick

A pinned element, a scroll-scrubbed clip-path, and a colour that shifts per frame. Copy it, remix it, ship it.
